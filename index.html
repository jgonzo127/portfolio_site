A Little Bit About Myself:

I'm a new Front End web designer interested in learning as much as I can about the art of coding. The idea of combining design and content on something as powerful and frequently used as the web is fascinating to me.

Working in the mysterious realm of the internet is an idea that dawned on me within the last year or so. I didn't realize how much time myself and everyone around me relied on the internet for so many things. Of course I need to learn how to make stuff on the internet!

Friendship, stability, and hard work are all very important to me. I feel that one's greatest challenge lies within one's self, and overcoming that challenge is the most rewarding of lifelong goals.
